About the Artist

My mandala journey began during the COVID-19 lockdown out of curiosity and a desire to learn a new art form. What started as a hobby soon turned into a deep passion. Through sleepless nights, symmetry, and mindful patterns, I explored mandala, dot mandala, and Lippan art as a form of artistic expression.

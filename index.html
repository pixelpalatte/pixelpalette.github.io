About Chromatic Circles

Chromatic Circles is a mindful art space dedicated to mandala, dot mandala, and Lippan art. Each piece is created slowly and intentionally, reflecting balance, harmony, and inner stillness through geometric expression.

About the Artist

I began my mandala journey during the COVID-19 lockdown out of curiosity and a desire to learn. What started as a hobby gradually evolved into a deeply meditative practice rooted in patience, symmetry, and mindful repetition.
